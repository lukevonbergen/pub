A classic British pub offering modern English cuisine in the heart of Marylebone

Nestled on a quiet corner of Marylebone High Street, The Crown & Thistle has been a beloved gathering place since 1892. Our Grade II listed building retains its original Victorian character, from the ornate ceiling roses to the mahogany bar where countless stories have been shared over perfectly poured pints.

Today, we honour that heritage while embracing modern British cooking, sourcing the finest seasonal ingredients from trusted suppliers across the British Isles.

"Cooked with charcoal, ember and flame"

Our kitchen is built around a custom-made charcoal grill, where prime cuts of British beef, whole fish from Cornish day boats, and seasonal vegetables are kissed by smoke and flame. We work closely with small farms and independent suppliers who share our commitment to sustainable, ethical sourcing.

Premium Beers, Fine Wines & Handcrafted Cocktails

Our bar showcases an exceptional collection of cask ales from Britain's finest independent breweries, alongside a carefully curated wine list featuring both Old World classics and exciting New World discoveries.

Our bartenders craft seasonal cocktails using house-made syrups, fresh herbs from our rooftop garden, and premium spirits from artisan distilleries.
